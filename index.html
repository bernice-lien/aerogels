About AeroPure

The Extraction of Contaminants in Water with Aerogel

At AeroPure, our vision is to ensure that every individual has access to clean water. 1 in 3 people globally does not have access to safe drinking water. That is around 2.2 billion people around the world. Of these individuals, 3,575,000 people die from water-related diseases. AeroPure takes a different approach by using cellulose and alginate aerogels as sorbents to remove contaminants from your water. We target areas where there is an absence of adequate filteration systems, or areas that use wells as their main source of water. Our goal is to distribute our AeroPure packets to individuals that don't have access to safe drinking water.

How AeroPures Work?

1

Fill

Fill your mesh bag with the AeroPure beads.
2

Soak

Then the AeroPure packet is soaked in the water, allowing the packet to remove contaminants in the water.
3

Wash and Squeeze

In order to regenerate the AeroPure beads, it must be placed in vinegar. Once the packet has been absorbed the contaminants. Squeeze the polluted substances out of the AeroPure beads.
3

Repeat

It can be used for 20 to 30 times without decrease in absorption capabilities.

Our Technology

Aerogels

Cellulose Aerogel

Cellulose Aerogel is the most abundant organic compound on Earth. Around 40% of plant biomass is cellulose, making it the most available biopolymer. In order to tackle one of the problems of waste biomass, AeroPure extracts cellulose from waste biomass to make cellulose aerogels.

Alginate Aerogel

Alginate has been used in aerogel fabrication for medical and non-medical applications. At AeroPure, we use Alginate to absorb toxic heavy metal ions. Heavy metals in water sources are the result of the direct or indirect release of metal from industrial settings.
