About Us



  • Aeropure was founded by 4 teens passionate about solving one of the world's biggest problems: the scarcity of clean water. We have the technology to provide people with drinkable water; however, some solutions that work for developed countries may not work for developing countries. We decided to take a different approach by using aerogel to make filteres that are cost-effective, biodegradable, and portable.
